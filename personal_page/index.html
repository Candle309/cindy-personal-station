Who I Am

When I was a little girl, I used to play video games with other boys and I always beat them. I was very proud that I played better because at that time, boys were supposed to be better at video games, but I always won. Starting from then, I tried learning techniques that were typically preferred by boys and programming was one of them. It has grown a lot of passion inside of me and now it has become something more than just a hobby. I am proud to be a woman engineer specifically a programmer and I believe programming changes the world.

What I Do

I try to learn new techniques and using them to build something I can think of all the time. Little achievements always put a big smile on my face. Besides the new things, digging up some old projects I did and perfecting them is also something I like to do.

Interests

In my spare time, I love playing video games. DOTA2 is one of my favorites. I enjoy the process of communicating and collaborating with others as well as sharing the success feeling among the team. Also, gym is where I spend a bunch of time. I believe engineers should be energetic all the time. And also girls should keep fitting. Cooking is also my love. I won't forget to attend cooking classes and planning meals for friends and family even though I'm busy. It is sweet to receive feedback and improve the recipes.
